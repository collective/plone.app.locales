2) Unwelcome terms

The following is the list of words in the target language that should not appear
in the translation. May be used as a notepad while translating, storing the
stubs of words that should be searched and replaced at the end of work:

     añadir        -> agregar
     borrar        -> eliminar
     compañía      -> empresa
     directorio    -> carpeta
     fichero       -> archivo
     lenguaje      -> idioma


3) Misc. language specific rules

Any translation notes you want to leave for future translators.

Spreadsheet should be translated as "Libro" when making reference to
Microsoft Excel documents, for respecting the well known and official existing
translation. However, when making reference to OpenOffice documents or any other
spreadsheet documents, the more generic term "Hoja de cálculo" should be used
instead.
